---

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Dashboard">
    Log in to iio and go to your main dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start New Doc Space">
    Click the "New Doc Space" button in the upper right.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Name & Confirm">
    Enter a name for your documentation and click "Create".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Begin Writing">
    Use the editor to add your first page, heading, or code block.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">Invite your team to collaborate from the Members tab any time.</Callout>
