## Quick Start

To create your first documentation space:

1. Sign in to your iio account
2. Click "New Doc Space" on your dashboard
3. Enter a name for your project
4. Click "Create"

That's it! Your new documentation space is ready to organize, edit, and share docs.

<Callout kind="tip">
You can add members or set permissions any time from your space settings.
</Callout>
