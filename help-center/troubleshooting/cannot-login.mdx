---
title: Cannot Log In to iio
description: Steps to resolve login problems in iio.
---

## Symptoms

- "Invalid email or password" message
- Login page reloads

## Solutions

<Steps>
  <Step title="Reset your password">
    Click "Forgot Password?" and follow the email link to reset.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check credentials">
    Ensure there are no typos in email or password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear browser cache">
    Log out, clear cache, and try again.
  </Step>
</Steps>
